kim@harilo.com

Sthapit - Co-founder

A shopping fiend who happens to have a PhD in Controls Engineering (Haptics/Robotics and Earthquake resistant buildings) and a minor in Math (numerics). He is an alumnus of Georgia Tech, MIT, and Berkeley and enjoys creating as well as consuming technology and solving interesting logistics problems. Enabling true online shopping from Nepal might just be the mother of all logistics problems so of course he had to be involved.

He single handedly coded Harilo from scratch on a dinky netbook because his Thinkpad broke (and no one in KTM could fix it) and in spite of all the power outages and internet downtimes and is very proud of it! For his next project he looks forward to coding with his hands tied behind his back and typing with his nose.

Above all he enjoys meeting new and interesting people - if you send him an email at sthapit@harilo.com he promises to answer it. For work queries please direct your questions at http://harilo.com/contact for faster responses.
